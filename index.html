Our Mission

Our mission is to bridge the gap between NGOs, facilitating collaboration, resource sharing, and amplified impact. We envisioned NGO Connect as a one-stop solution for streamlining communication among NGOs, enhancing resource utilization and optimization, fostering meaningful partnerships and networks, amplifying social impact and change. As students of BTech, we recognized the potential of technology in driving social change. With NGO Connect, we aim to empower NGOs to reach a wider audience, simplify resource allocation and distribution, create a community that inspires and supports each other. Join us in our journey to make a difference. Together, let's connect, collaborate, and create lasting impact.
